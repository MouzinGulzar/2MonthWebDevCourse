MY FAVOURITE HOBBY

BAKING

Baking is something I really enjoy. It's not too hard, and I'm no expert in it either, but it's a lot of fun. You take stuff like flour, sugar, eggs, and mix them up. Then, you put it in the oven, and magic happens. The whole house smells amazing. I may not be a fancy chef, but I make some tasty stuff. Baking makes me feel good, and I like to share the things I bake with my friends and family. It's a simple joy in life, and I wouldn't trade it for anything.
